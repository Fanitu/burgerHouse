Burger House FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions about our delicious burgers and services

Where is Burger House located?

We're located at 123 Tasty Avenue, Food District, Flavor City. We're right in the heart of the city, easily accessible from all major areas. We also have free parking available for our dine-in customers!

How long does delivery take?

Our standard delivery time is 25-40 minutes depending on your location and current order volume. During peak hours, it might take up to 50 minutes. We always strive to deliver your food hot and fresh!

Do you have vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer 3 delicious vegetarian burger options including our famous Portobello Mushroom Burger and Spicy Black Bean Burger. All our vegetarian options can be customized with a variety of fresh toppings.

What are your operating hours?

We're open Monday to Saturday from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and Sundays from 12:00 PM to 10:00 PM. Our kitchen stops taking orders 30 minutes before closing time. Holiday hours may vary.

Still have questions? Contact us directly and we'll be happy to help!
